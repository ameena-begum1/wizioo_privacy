01

Introduction

Wizioo.Ai is an AI-powered chat application built for Android. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is, and isn't, handled when you use the app. We've kept it as straightforward as possible.

Using Wizioo.Ai means you agree to this policy. If you have any questions, you can always reach us at the contact information below.

02

What we collect

Almost nothing. Here's the full picture:

  • No account or sign-in is required to use Wizioo.Ai.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not run analytics or track how you use the app.
  • We do not serve ads or share data with advertisers.
Your conversation history is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. It is never uploaded to any server operated by Wizioo.Ai.

03

Microphone & voice

Wizioo.Ai includes a voice input feature that converts your speech to text. Microphone access is requested only when you tap the voice button.

  • Speech recognition is handled by Android's on-device Speech Recognition engine.
  • No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted by Wizioo.Ai.
  • You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings.

04

Google Gemini API

Wizioo.Ai uses Google Gemini 2.0 to generate responses to your messages. When you send a message, its text is transmitted to Google's servers to produce a reply.

  • This transmission is necessary for the core functionality of the app.
  • Data sent to Gemini is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Wizioo.Ai does not log, store, or have access to the content sent to or received from Gemini.
We recommend not sharing sensitive personal information such as passwords, financial details, or ID numbers, in your chats.

05

Data storage & security

Your chat history is stored locally on your device. It is not synced to the cloud or backed up to any server by Wizioo.Ai.

  • You can delete your history at any time from within the app.
  • Uninstalling Wizioo.Ai will permanently remove all locally stored data.

06

Third-party services

Wizioo.Ai integrates with one external service:

  • Google Gemini API - used to generate AI responses. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks are used.

07

Children's privacy

Wizioo.Ai is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe your child has used this app and has a privacy concern, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

08

Changes to this policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. When we do, we'll update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of Wizioo.Ai after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
